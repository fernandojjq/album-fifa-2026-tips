Expert strategies that help thousands of collectors finish their albums without spending a fortune

Buy in Bulk Packs

Purchase starter packs first, then buy individual packets of teams you need most. Focus on high-demand teams like Brazil, Argentina, and USA for easier trading.

Facebook groups, Reddit communities, and local clubs are goldmines for trading. The more people you connect with, the faster you complete your album.

Use a tracking app to know exactly which stickers you need. SWAP26 lets you track your collection and find matching trades automatically.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick answers to the most common questions about the FIFA 2026 album

The official Panini FIFA 2026 album contains ~980 stickers: ~960 team stickers (~20 per team for all 48 national teams) and ~20 tournament/special stickers. With optional extras (promo stickers, variants), you may reach ~1,012-1,014 total, but these are NOT required for album completion.
FIFA 2026 stickers are OUT NOW in most countries! The album and packets are available at major retailers, online stores, and Panini's official channels. Starter packs, collectors' boxes, and individual packets are widely available. Get yours before they sell out!
We recommend using SWAP26 (swap26.pages.dev), a free PWA that lets you scan or manually add stickers, tracks your collection in real-time, and automatically finds trades with other collectors. It works offline with QR codes and syncs across all your devices.
The hardest stickers to find are foil/shiny stickers (special finishes on badges, logos, and tournament elements) and promo stickers from regional distributions (like Coca-Cola promos). Note: Panini does NOT use numbered limited editions like trading cards — there are no 1/100 or 1/500 numbered stickers in the standard album.
Individual packets are almost always cheaper because complete sets from Panini or third parties come with a premium. However, packets involve randomness. Our strategy: buy packets strategically, track what you have, and trade duplicates to other collectors to minimize costs.
